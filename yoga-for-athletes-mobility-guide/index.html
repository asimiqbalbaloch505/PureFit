Yoga for Lifters: The Science of Restoring Power

Category: Mobility & Recovery • 6 min read

Heavy squats aur deadlifts aapke muscles ko "tight" aur stiff bana dete hain. Agar aapka Range of Motion (ROM) restrict ho jaye, toh aapka injury risk badh jata hai aur muscle fiber recruitment kam ho jati hai. Yoga sirf stretching nahi hai; yeh aapke Myofascial Tissue ko release karne ka ek systematic tarika hai.

1. Vagus Nerve aur Recovery Acceleration

Yoga mein Diaphragmatic Breathing ka istemal hota hai jo aapke Vagus Nerve ko activate karta hai. Yeh nerve aapki body ko "Fight or Flight" mode se nikal kar "Rest and Digest" (Parasympathetic) mode mein daalti hai, jisse workout ke baad recovery 2x fast ho jati hai.

Pro-Tip: Yoga session ke dauran nasal breathing (naak se saans lena) hi karein. Yeh nitric oxide level badhata hai aur oxygen delivery ko optimize karta hai.
