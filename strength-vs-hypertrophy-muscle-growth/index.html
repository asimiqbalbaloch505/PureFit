Strength vs. Hypertrophy: Decoding Muscle Growth

Category: Exercise Science • 7 min read

Kya aapne kabhi socha hai ki ek powerlifter ek bodybuilder se zyada weight kaise utha leta hai, bhale hi uska muscle size chota ho? Iska jawab hai Neurological Adaptation vs. Physiological Growth. Dono goals ke liye training variables bilkul alag hote hain.

1. Raw Strength: The Power of the Nervous System

Strength training ka main goal hai aapke Central Nervous System (CNS) ko train karna taaki woh ek saath zyada se zyada muscle fibers ko recruit kar sake. Isse Myofibrillar Hypertrophy hoti hai—jahan muscle fibers khud dense aur mazboot bante hain.

ATP-CP Recovery: Heavier loads (85%+ of 1RM) lift karne ke baad aapke cells ko 3-5 minutes lagte hain apna energy store (Phosphocreatine) refill karne mein. Isliye strength training mein rest intervals lambe hote hain.

2. Hypertrophy: Building the "Size"

Muscle size badhane ke liye hum Sarcoplasmic Hypertrophy par focus karte hain. Iska matlab hai muscle cells ke andar ka volume (fluid, glycogen, aur mitochondria) badhana. Iske liye Mechanical Tension aur Metabolic Stress (woh "pump" wali feeling) zaroori hai.
