Supplementation: What Actually Works and What is Hype?

Category: Supplements • 5 min read

95% of your results come from food and training. Evidence-Based Supplementation provides that final 5% edge for those looking for elite performance.

The Gold Standard: Research-Backed Essentials

Only a handful of products are backed by extensive scientific research. These focus on Bioavailability and cellular energy.

  • Creatine Monohydrate: Increases ATP production for power output.
  • Whey Protein: High-leucine source for post-workout repair.
  • Vitamin D3 & K2: Essential for Bone Mineral Density.
  • Magnesium: Critical for muscle relaxation and sleep.

Simple Supplement Stack Guide

Supplement Recommended Dose Main Goal
Creatine 5g Daily Strength & Power
Whey Protein 20-30g as needed Muscle Repair
Magnesium 200-400mg Recovery & Sleep

Always consult with a professional before starting new regimens. Keep your stack clean and evidence-based for the best long-term results!
