HIIT: Maximize Fat Burn in Just 20 Minutes

Category: Cardio & Fat Loss • 5 min read

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) cardiovascular fitness ka gold standard hai. Yeh training aapke **Anaerobic Threshold** ko push karti hai, jisse body workout ke baad bhi calories burn karti rehti hai.

EPOC: The 'Afterburn' Explained

HIIT ka asli jaadu EPOC (Excess Post-exercise Oxygen Consumption) mein hai. Jab aap 90% heart rate par kaam karte hain, toh body mein "oxygen debt" create hota hai. Is debt ko recover karne ke liye aapka metabolism workout ke 24-48 ghante baad tak high rehta hai.

Pro Tip: Agar aap workout ke baad breath restore karne mein 5-10 minute le rahe hain, toh samajh lijiye aapne EPOC successfully trigger kar diya hai.
