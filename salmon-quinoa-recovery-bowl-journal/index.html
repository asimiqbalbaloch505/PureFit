The Ultimate Recovery Bowl: My Go-To Post-Workout Meal

Published: May 05, 2026 • Journal Entry #4

Training ke baad aapki body ek sensitive state mein hoti hai jise hum "Anabolic Window" kehte hain. Is waqt aap jo khate hain, woh tay karta hai ki aap recover honge ya sirf thake hue rahenge. Mera Salmon aur Quinoa bowl Nutrient Partitioning ke liye optimized hai—yani yeh calories ko fat storage se hata kar muscle repair ki taraf bhejta hai.

1. Omega-3 aur DOMS Reduction

Salmon mein mojud EPA aur DHA (Omega-3 fatty acids) natural anti-inflammatory agents hain. Yeh workout se hone wale oxidative stress ko kam karte hain, jisse Delayed Onset Muscle Soreness (DOMS) yani agle din ka dard kaafi kam ho jata hai.

2. Glycogen Refilling

Weight lifting aapke muscles mein jama glycogen (sugar storage) ko khatam kar deta hai. Quinoa ek perfect Low-Glycemic carbohydrate hai jo blood sugar ko spike kiye bina aapke energy stores ko refill karta hai. Isse aapko workout ke baad wali "crash" feel nahi hoti.

Yaad rakhein: Workout toh sirf 1 ghante ka hota hai, asli transformation baki ke 23 ghanton ki nutrition aur sleep par depend karti hai.
