Protein: The Building Block of Muscle and Metabolism

Category: Nutrition Science • 6 min read

Protein sirf bodybuilders ke liye nahi hai. Yeh aapke har ek cell ki repair, enzymes ki production aur immune system ke liye essential hai. Jab aap weight training karte hain, toh protein Muscle Protein Synthesis (MPS) ko trigger karta hai, jo recovery ka main engine hai.

1. The Metabolic Edge: TEF

Protein ka Thermic Effect of Food (TEF) sabse zyada hota hai. Iska matlab hai ki agar aap 100 calorie protein khate hain, toh body lagbhag 25-30 calories sirf usse digest karne mein burn kar deti hai. Yeh fats aur carbs ke muqable kahin zyada efficient hai fat loss ke liye.

The Leucine Threshold: Muscle building switch on karne ke liye har meal mein kam se kam 2.5g - 3g Leucine (ek amino acid) hona zaroori hai. Iske bina MPS optimal level par start nahi hoti.

2. Bioavailability aur Quality

Saara protein ek barabar nahi hota. DIAAS (Digestible Indispensable Amino Acid Score) se pata chalta hai ki aapki body kitna protein absorb kar sakti hai. Animal sources typically plant sources se zyada "bioavailable" hote hain.

Protein SourceBioavailabilityKey Nutrient
Whey IsolateExtremely HighRapid Leucine release
Whole EggsVery HighCholine & Healthy Fats
Chicken/FishHighComplete Amino Profile
Lentils/TofuModerateFiber & Phyto-nutrients

Din bhar ka total protein intake sabse bada factor hai. Target karein **1.6g se 2.2g per kg of bodyweight** agar aap active hain aur muscle build karna chahte hain.
