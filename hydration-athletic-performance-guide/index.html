Hydration & Athletic Performance: More Than Just Water

Category: Performance Science • 5 min read

Hydration ko aksar ignore kiya jata hai, lekin performance par iska impact massive hota hai. Sirf 2% **Body Mass Loss** (dehydration ke wajah se) aapke cognitive focus aur muscular endurance ko 10-20% tak gira sakta hai.

1. Plasma Volume aur Cardiovascular Strain

Jab aap dehydrated hote hain, toh aapka Plasma Volume (blood ka liquid part) kam ho jata hai. Isse blood "viscous" ya gaadha ho jata hai. Aapka heart rate upar jata hai kyunki use gaadhe blood ko pump karne ke liye zyada mehnat karni padti hai. Is process ko Cardiovascular Drift kehte hain.

Why it matters: Thicker blood means slower oxygen delivery to the muscles and slower removal of metabolic waste like CO2 and Lactic Acid.

2. Electrolyte Homeostasis: The Spark Plugs

Muscle contraction ke liye electrical signals zaroori hain, jo Sodium, Potassium aur Magnesium carry karte hain. Sirf plain water peene se aapka blood dilute ho sakta hai (Hyponatremia), jo cramping aur weakness ka sabse bada kaaran hai.
