The Truth About Intermittent Fasting: Science and Strategy

Category: Dietary Protocols • 6 min read

Intermittent fasting is a method of Circadian Rhythm Alignment that restricts eating to a specific window. This approach isn't just about calories; it's about optimizing your Insulin Sensitivity.

How Fasting Triggers Fat Loss

When insulin levels drop during the fasting window, your body enters Lipolysis, mobilizing stored fat for energy.

  • The 16:8 Method: Fasting for 16 hours and eating during an 8-hour window.
  • Metabolic Flexibility: Switching efficiently between burning carbs and fat.
  • Reduced Inflammation: Lowering oxidative stress markers.

The Power of Autophagy

One of the most profound benefits is Autophagy—a cellular "housekeeping" process where your body cleans out damaged cells and regenerates new ones.
