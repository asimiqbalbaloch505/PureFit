Intermittent Fasting: Science and Strategy

Category: Dietary Protocols • 6 min read

Intermittent Fasting (IF) sirf "kab khana hai" ka sawal nahi hai, balki yeh aapke body ke Hormonal Environment ko optimize karne ka ek tarika hai. Jab aap fasting state mein hote hain, aapka body Insulin levels ko drastically drop karta hai, jisse fat cells se energy nikalna (Lipolysis) asan ho jata hai.

1. Autophagy: Cellular Self-Cleaning

Fasting ka sabse bada benefit hai Autophagy. Yeh ek biological process hai jahan cells apne purane aur damaged proteins ko "recycle" karte hain. Yeh process tabhi trigger hota hai jab mTOR (growth signal) low hota hai aur nutrient intake band hota hai.

The 18-Hour Mark: Deep autophagy aam taur par 16-18 ghante ki fasting ke baad peak karti hai. Is dauran aapka body "anti-aging" mode mein hota hai.

2. Metabolic Flexibility

IF aapki body ko "Fat Adapted" banata hai. Iska matlab hai ki aapka system efficient ho jata hai carbs aur fats ke beech switch karne mein. Jab glycogen khatam hota hai, body Ketones produce karti hai, jo brain ke liye ek superior fuel source hain.
